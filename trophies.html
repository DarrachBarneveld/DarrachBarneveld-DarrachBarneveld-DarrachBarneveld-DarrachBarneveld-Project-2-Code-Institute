HOW TO PLAY

  1. Click on the circle to select a segment
  2. Each segment will have questions related to a category
  3. Choose the difficulty of the questions on the next screen
  4. Depending on difficulty you will be awarded medals of various denomination
  5. To unlock a medal you must score 8 points or higher during the quiz
  6. Collect all medals of a category to unlock the trivia trump card
  7. Collect all trivia trump card trophies to win!
